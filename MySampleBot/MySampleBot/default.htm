Sample Calculator Bot

This is an intelligent Bot which can parse your text and does arithmetic operations. Currently it supports add, subtract, multiply and divide on integers.

Skype integration
Option 1: Click to add bot as a contact in your skype account. (In browser, skype website might ask you to signin to your account)
(Or)
Option 2: In your "Skype for Desktop", click 'add bots icon', search for "MySampleBotJKLocal" Bot and add as a contact. Help
Help
It expects 3 or 4 words, it includes 2 numbers and 1 operator.
(4th can be a comma(,), dot(.), :, ; as separator between numbers)
Below are sample commands,
Hi
Help
add 1, 2
multiply 3 6
add: 3 6
19-1
4 * 6
120/2
